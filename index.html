FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and process.

How long does it take to build a website?
The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. A basic 5-page website typically takes 7–10 business days. A custom website or e-commerce store can take 3–6 weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed project timeline based on your specific requirements. We always deliver on time without compromising quality.
Do you provide website hosting and domain registration?
While we don't directly sell hosting or domains, we provide expert guidance on the best hosting providers for your needs (such as Hostinger, Bluehost, SiteGround, or AWS) and assist with the complete setup, DNS configuration, and domain pointing. We ensure your website is live and running smoothly.
Will my website be mobile-friendly and responsive?
Absolutely! Every website we build is 100% mobile-responsive by default. We follow a mobile-first design approach, ensuring your site looks perfect and functions flawlessly on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops of all screen sizes. We test across multiple devices and browsers before delivery.
Can I update the website content myself after launch?
Yes! For WordPress and CMS-based websites, we provide full admin access and a training session so you can easily update text, images, products, and blog posts yourself. We also provide documentation. For custom-coded sites, we can add a CMS or provide ongoing content management as part of our maintenance packages.
What information do you need to get started?
To get started, we typically need: your business overview and goals, any brand assets (logo, brand colors, fonts), reference websites you like, content (text, images, videos), and specific features or functionality requirements. Don't worry if you don't have everything — our team helps you gather and organize all materials during the discovery phase.
Do you offer ongoing website maintenance and support?
Yes, we offer comprehensive website maintenance and support packages. All our plans come with a free support period (1–6 months depending on the plan). After that, we offer affordable monthly maintenance packages that include plugin/theme updates, security monitoring, regular backups, performance optimization, and priority technical support.
How does the payment structure work?
We work with a simple milestone-based payment structure: 50% advance payment to initiate the project, 25% upon design approval, and the remaining 25% upon final delivery and launch. We accept payments via bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), UPI, Razorpay, and international wire transfers for overseas clients.
What makes Zenova Group different from other web agencies?
What sets us apart is our commitment to results, not just aesthetics. Every website we build is strategically crafted to achieve your business goals — whether that's generating leads, driving sales, or building brand authority. We combine beautiful design, clean code, SEO best practices, and ongoing support into every project. Plus, we treat every client as a long-term partner, not just a transaction.
